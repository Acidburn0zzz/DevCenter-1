Overview

Build or customize mobile apps that extend the scope of your Maximo enterprise system by providing mobile users with protected access to data and services any time, anywhere in both connected and disconnected scenarios. Worklight V6.1.0.1 can be used with Maximo Anywhere V7.5.1. (Worklight Foundation V6.2 and MobileFirst Platform Foundation V6.3 are not supported in the current release of Maximo Anywhere, V7.5.1.)
